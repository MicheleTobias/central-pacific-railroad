Central Pacific Railroad Route Logic

The goal of this project is to use primary map sources to better understand the history and route logic of the route of the Central Pacific Railroad.

Mapping the Planned Route of the Central Pacific Railroad

The railroad lines on this map were digitized from historical maps that our team sourced, had scanned, and georeferenced (as needed).
